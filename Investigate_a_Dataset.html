Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

this a data set of medical appointments in Brazil, our main goal is exploring this dataset to know why patients didn't show up in their appointments, the data set contains the following columns:

  • PatientId : unique identifier for the patient
  • AppointmentID : unique identifier for the appointment
  • Gender : Gender of the patient
  • ScheduledDay: the day secheduled for appointment
  • AppointmentDay: the actual day the patient shown up in it
  • Age : Age of the patient
  • Neighbourhood : the place of the appointment
  • Scholarship : is the patient registered in a wellfair program or not
  • Hipertension : is the patient has Hipertension
  • Diabetes : is the patient has Diabetes
  • Alcoholism : is the patient has Alcoholism
  • Handcap : is the patient Handcap
  • SMS_received : is the paient receive sms for the appointment
  • No-show : is the patient show up in his appointment (no for show up - yes for not show)

Question(s) for Analysis¶

The main goal of this analysis is to answer the question why patients didn't show up in their appointments, what is the factors that prevent them form showing up in order to be able to predict their behaviuor in the future depeing on these features
